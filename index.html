Geovisualization Portfolio GEOG 3540

Percentage of Population 25-44 by County, Illinois

This project depicts the percentage of the population ranging in age from 25 to 44 years old in each county of Illinois. Counties with a higher percentage seem to be near large urban areas, such as Chicago and St. Louis. This project was a choropleth mapping assignment for GEOG:3540.

Airport/Airfield Count per County, Texas

This project depicts the number of airports/airfields per county in Texas via graduated symbols. Counties with a higher count of airports/airfields coincide with large urban areas and major lines of communication. This project was created for GEOG:3540.

Percentages of Population Under 18 Yrs Old & Over 65 Yrs Old per County, North Carolina

This project compares high, medium, and low percentages of the population both under 18 years old and over 65 years old. Counties with a "younger" population appear near the interior of the state, while counties with an "older" population border the exterior of the state, for the most part. This project was created as a multivariate mapping assignment for GEOG:3540.

Population and Housing in Turkey

This project provides a small multiples view of four choropleth maps covering population density, percentage of population aged 25-64, percentage of single-family housing units, and percentage of extended-family housing units in Turkey, by Province. This project was created for GEOG:3540.
